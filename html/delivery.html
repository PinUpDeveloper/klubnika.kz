Delivery Terms

We deliver daily, keeping proper temperature control. Minimum order — 3,000 ₸.

Zone A — city center

Time: 1–2 hours

Price: free from 8,000 ₸, otherwise 900 ₸

Zone B — residential areas

Time: 2–3 hours

Price: free from 12,000 ₸, otherwise 1,200 ₸

Zone C — suburbs

Time: 3–5 hours

Price: 1,800 ₸ (fixed)

Delivery Slots

  • Courier calls 10–15 minutes before arrival.
  • Fragile products are packed separately.
  • Pickup: Mon–Sun, 10:00–20:00 (0 ₸).
  • Payment: cash, card, Kaspi.
What if I’m not at home?

We can reschedule or leave the order at reception/neighbors with your approval.

How do you store chilled items?

Thermal bags and ice packs keep products fresh until handover.

Can I change the address after placing an order?

Yes, if the order hasn’t been handed to the courier. Contact us on the Contact page.
